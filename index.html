What is the Expenses App

Our brand new receipt app will allow employees to enter information on their purchases quickly and easily, giving you more time to work on the projects at hand. Save time! Save paper!

Login Page

Our home-page is simple in design, to allow you to access the app with ease and minimum delay.
Logging in has never been easier. Using Google Sign-In, you won't need to remember individual usernames and passwords, just login using your Google account.

Approve Expense Reports

Managers are able to quickly Approve Submitted Expense reports.
Managers are able to Search by Expense names or by the Name of users to be able to quickly filter through results and approve reports.

Reports

No need to spend hours writing paragraphs for your expenses. With our app you can quickly fill in text boxes specifically for appropriate fields. After submitting your report, it is automatically sent to your Manager. Unfinished reports are displayed in the homepage of the user's account.
